Our Story

A Neighborhood Gathering Place

Rooted in Pitsburg, Ohio since 2025, Tin Roof Tavern was born from a simple belief: great food and cold drinks taste better shared.

We're your neighborhood gathering place — a full kitchen turning out real comfort food alongside a full bar stocked with craft and classic pours. Whether you're catching the game, celebrating something special, or just unwinding after a long week, there's always a seat for you under our roof.
